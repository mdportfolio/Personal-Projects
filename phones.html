History of Smartphones

The world's first smartphone was the IBM Simon which was released in 1994. In 1996, Nokia introduced the Nokia 9000 Communicator. Microsoft created Windows Mobile in 2000. In 2006, the Blackberry came and took the world by storm. Then a year later, the landscape changed completely with the first iPhone. Then in 2008 the Android was introduced to the world. Now iPhone and Android smartphones are the top selling phones on the market. Here are a list of features that smartphones use today.

  • Long-lasting battery
  • Warp-speed processing
  • Crystal-clear display
  • Great camera
  • Plenty of storage
  • Infrared remote control
  • Wireless Charging
  • Fingerprint sensor
